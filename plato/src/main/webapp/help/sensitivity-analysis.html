Sensibility analysis of importance factors

 

This page explains the sensitivity analysis of importance factors of objective trees in Plato.

Sensitivity analysis

The resulting ordering of the alternatives denpends on the test results and the importance factors. Sometimes even a small change in the importance factors can lead to a different ordering. The goal of the sensitivity analysis is to discover such unstable importance factors.

Results

The results of the sensitivity analysis are displayed in an objective tree with the values aggregated as weighted sum.
Nodes with unstable importance factors are highlighted (red color). This means that even small changes to the weights of this node can change the ordering of the alternatives.
Nodes that are not sensitive themself but have direct or indirect sensitive children are also highlighted (pink color).
